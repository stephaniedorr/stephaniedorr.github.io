My story

Hi, I'm Stephanie. I can't remember a time when I wasn't curious about something. I've always needed to understand the story behind the story.

During college, I seized the opportunity to explore and travel by becoming an international exchange student. Terrified to be leaving my family and traveling halfway around the globe, I became more at ease as I met people, heard their stories, and shared my own. The more stories I heard, the more I wanted to investigate.

After college, I married an Army officer and spent the next 27 years traveling the world. I have had the honor of hearing the stories of more people than I can possibly count. Along the way, my own life story has taken many turns. I have been an event planner, a philanthropist, and an educator. Eighteen household moves have made me an expert logistician. My husband's four overseas deployments have made me resilient, resourceful and independent.

Throughout the twists and turns of my personal life, my love for all things technology has remained a constant. I wrote my first bits of code in high school. As an exchange student, I used the early Internet to communicate with my professors in the United States. As a young mom, every ounce of education technology I could get my hands on. As an Army wife, I designed and published newsletters, cookbooks, and online blogs.

As we approach our 27th anniversary in the military I am leaning forward to the next chapter of my story. After extensive research, I found Bloc. As a UX Design Apprentice at Bloc, I’ve learned how to be a better problem-solver. By combining design science with a beautiful design I’m able to create a tested and effective user-focused digital experience.

Every design tells a story. Tell me your story and together we can share it with the world.
