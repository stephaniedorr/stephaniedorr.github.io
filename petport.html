THE CHALLENGE

Busy lives make staying organized a challenge.

Much care, love, and attention goes into our fury
friends. Grooming, boarding, and routine doctor’s visits
ensure that our furry friends stay healthy.

Sometimes things go wrong and we find ourselves
looking into the eyes of an injured or ailing buddy.
It’s important to be prepared for times of emergencies

THE SOLUTION

Streamline the organizational process.

Pet Port streamlines the search process, organizes medical records,
and allows you to set vaccination and appointment reminders.

Research

Get to know our users.

In order to understand how pet owners currently
handled their pet’s medical records, booked vet
and grooming appointments, and managed the
care and health of their pet I developed and
distributed a survey across social media channels.
This survey aimed to specifically find out:

  • Would the pet owner utilize emergency facilities?
  • What about on the weekend or after hours?
  • Can pet owners locate their closest emergency facility?
  • How many pet owners have their pet groomed regulary?
  • Can pet owners get to their pet's records quickly?
  • In 2016 Americans spent $66.75 billion on their pets.
  • In 2016 $22,25 billion went to veterinary services, supplies, & OTC meds.
  • 100% of respondents said they would utilize an emergency facility.
  • 39% stated they didn’t know where their nearest emergency was located.
  • 44% stated they could not put their hands on their pet’s medical records.
  • 15% of respondents said they regularly use a groomer.

The picture was clear, we spend money on our pets
(sometimes big money) and we could use a little help
connecting with quality veterinarian professionals as well as
keeping our pet’s medical records handy and organized.

PERSONAS

Interviewing & surveying

I conducted one on one interviews with my survey-takers
to delve deeper into their individual wants and needs.

Thru these interviews three strong user personalities emerged:
The Tech-Savvy Professional, The Busy Planner and the Thrifty Student.
